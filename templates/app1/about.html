Bangladesh Travel Magazine

Bangladesh Travel Magazine is a travellers community, where each traveller can help others to explore natural beauty of Bangladesh.

Bangladesh is a land of beauty. There are many places around our country which are beautiful but yet not known to many travellers. Also certain places are more suitable for journey in certain season. Bangladesh Travel Magazine aims to let you know what places are being most travelled in recent times and how a journey should be made to these places
